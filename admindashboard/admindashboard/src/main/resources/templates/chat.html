Most Frequently Asked

Weekly timesheets must be submitted by Monday at 10:00 AM IST for the previous week. To submit, navigate to the Timesheets module, enter your hours, and click "Submit". If you miss the deadline, the timesheet will lock and you will need to request manager approval to unlock it.

Navigate to Leave Management > Apply Leave. Select your leave type (e.g., Casual, Sick), choose your start and end dates, and provide a brief reason. Once submitted, a notification is sent to your reporting manager for approval. You can track the status in the Leave History tab.

You can update your personal information by clicking your avatar in the top right and selecting My Profile. You have direct access to edit your emergency contacts and current address. Note: Changes to your legal name, PAN, or bank details require HR verification.

Monthly payslips are typically generated by the 3rd working day of the new month. To view or download them, navigate to the Payroll module and select Payslips. Choose the desired month and year from the dropdown menu, and click the download icon to save it as a PDF.
